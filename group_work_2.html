Group Task 2

Duration:
20
Grouping:
pairs or a group of three

Group Work Instructions

  1. Find job advertisement from internet (google/facebook/youtube/instagram, etc)
  2. install Intelligent CV - CV Maker from google play store
  3. Make cover letter/Application and Curriculum Vitae (CV) using Intelligent CV - CV Maker
  4. send your application letter and CV to your teacher's mail (wahyu@smkn1arahan.sch.id)
  5. Post your work in your social media (facebook, tiktok, instagram, etc)
